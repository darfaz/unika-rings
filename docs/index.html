Meet the Maker
"I've been reimagining the world around me since before I could walk. This is just the first time I'm inviting everyone else in."

— Nika, 22 · Founder & Jeweler

At ten months old, Nika turned her parents' walls into her first canvas. Before she was two, she was cutting the cords off window shades — not breaking things, inventing things. She's always seen the world a little differently.

That instinct never left. She grew up haunting vintage shops, turning secondhand finds into one-of-a-kind pieces, creating jewelry and clothing that nobody else had — because nobody else could see what she saw.

When she began her apprenticeship at Haricot Vert under Kelsey Armstrong — the collage artist and jewelry designer whose charm pieces are carried by MoMA — the raw creativity finally met real craft. Traditional metalsmithing. Bezel setting. The art of turning found objects into wearable stories. Nika went from someone who made beautiful things to someone who made them last.

Unika was born the day she looked down at her Oura Ring — a $400 piece of technology that looked like… nothing — and thought: I can fix that. One sterling silver jacket with a pink tourmaline later, her friends wouldn't stop asking where they could get one.

Now you can too.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is a ring jacket?+
A ring jacket is a piece of jewelry that wraps around an existing ring to transform its appearance. Unika ring jackets are designed specifically for the Oura smart ring — they slide onto the exterior, turning your health tracker into fine jewelry without affecting any sensors.
What materials are used?+
Every Unika ring jacket is handcrafted from .925 sterling silver with genuine bezel-set gemstones. We use traditional metalsmithing techniques — stones are bezel-set, never glued. Each piece is anti-tarnish treated for lasting beauty.
Will it affect my Oura Ring's sensors?+
No. The jacket hugs the outer surface of your ring, leaving all internal sensors (LED, temperature, accelerometer) completely unobstructed. Sleep tracking, HRV, heart rate, temperature — everything works exactly the same.
Which Oura Ring models are compatible?+
Unika ring jackets are compatible with Oura Ring Gen 3 and Gen 4, all sizes. This includes Heritage, Horizon, and all finish variants.
How is this different from silicone covers?+
Completely different category. Silicone covers are $7–15 plastic sleeves. Unika ring jackets are genuine sterling silver jewelry with real gemstones, handcrafted one at a time by a jeweler. It's the difference between a phone case and a watch.
