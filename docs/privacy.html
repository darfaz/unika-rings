Overview

Unika Rings ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit unikarings.com ("Site") and use our services.

We automatically collect:

How We Use Your Information

Third Parties

We share data only with:

We never sell your personal information.
